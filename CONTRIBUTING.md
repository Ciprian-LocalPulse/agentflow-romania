# CONTRIBUTING

Thank you for your interest in AgentFlow România.

## Contribution Rules

All external contributions are subject to review.

## Accepted Areas

- bug reports
- UI suggestions
- documentation improvements
- non-commercial enhancements

## Not Accepted Without Approval

- rebranding
- commercial forks
- resale clones
- unauthorized derivatives

## Process

1. Open an issue
2. Describe proposal clearly
3. Wait for approval before large changes
